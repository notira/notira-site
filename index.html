Never Miss a Patient Call Again.

Notira provides automated missed-call SMS responses for clinics and small businesses to improve customer communication and appointment follow-ups.

How Notira Works

Notira integrates with your business phone line. When a call is missed, our system automatically sends a compliant SMS message to the caller offering assistance, appointment scheduling, or follow-up support.

All messaging follows carrier guidelines and requires customer opt-in.

SMS Messaging Disclosure

By providing your phone number to a business using Notira’s services, you consent to receive SMS messages related to appointment reminders, missed call follow-ups, and customer support communications.

Message frequency varies depending on interaction. Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP or contact support@notira.co.
